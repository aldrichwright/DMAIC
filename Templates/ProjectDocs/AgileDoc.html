Sprint Retrospective


A Sprint Retrospective is a meeting that takes places at the end of sprint. The Team reflects on what went well and what went poorly.

A Sprint Retrospective involves the following people:

  • The Scrum Master
  • The Product Owner
  • The Team Members

and involves the following steps:

  • Set the Stage
  • Gather Data
  • Generate Ideas
  • Decide what to do
  • Close out the retrospective

All appropriate stakeholders should be notified of the results of the retrospective.
