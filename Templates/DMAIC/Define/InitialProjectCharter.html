Initial Project Charter




The Initial Project Charter outlines a Process Improvement Project, including the goals and roles within the project. Sections within the Project Charter include:

Section

Description
Project Name the Name of the Data Quality Project
Project Sponsors the Business Sponsors of the Project
Business Case

  		the Business Case for the Project. In other words, why is this project being done? This may include the ROI, or measurements such as cycle time may be enough
Project Manager The Project Manager who will manage the Project
Team Members The people who will work the Project
Business Justification The business reasons this project is being done, decreased expenses, reduced cycle time etc
Objectives

The specific things to be achieved; reduce defects in a certain process by x percentage etc
Deliverable

What will be delivered out of this project- changed processes and documentation, additional controls etc.
Scope
What will and won't be improved; this process or business area and not that one etc.
Constraints
Constraints on Project- Time, Budget, Personnel etc
